A Journey of Growth

Colours of Courage is a story-driven adventure that blends meaningful gameplay with the science of positive psychology.

You follow Sky, navigating the challenges of growing up, helping others, and learning how emotions shape the world.

This experience is not about being told what to do - it is about discovering growth through action, kindness, and reflection.

The Worlds of Colours of Courage

Set in the vibrant community of New Town and its symbolic landscapes, every location reflects emotional states and personal challenges. The following worlds include the Forest of Doubts, and Evershade, the original town Sky came from.

From peaceful natural areas to meaningful interactive spaces, the world evolves alongside your journey.
