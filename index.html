A Journey of Growth

The Colours of Courage is a story-driven adventure that blends meaningful gameplay with the science of positive psychology.

You follow Sky, navigating the challenges of growing up, helping others, and learning how emotions shape the way we see the world.

This experience is not about being told what to do — it is about discovering growth through action, kindness, and reflection.

The World of New Town

Set in the vibrant community of New Town and its symbolic landscapes, every location reflects emotional states and personal challenges.

From peaceful natural areas to meaningful interactive spaces, the world evolves alongside your journey.
